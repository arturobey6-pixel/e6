Our story

Born at dusk, on purpose

LuxuryRoomDesign began in 2017 when our founder, Isabelle Marchetti, noticed that every room she loved had been photographed at noon and lived in at night — and that almost nobody designed for the difference.

So the atelier made dusk its discipline. We choose fabrics under lamplight, test paint at 7 pm, and time every reveal for the hour the room will actually be lived in. The portfolio that followed — 120 rooms, 14 awards — is really one idea, repeated with conviction.

  • Materials with a second face. Everything we specify is chosen twice: once for daylight, once for lamplight.
  • Margins printed, always. Every invoice shows cost and our margin, line by line. Trust is a design material too.
  • One room at a time. Depth over breadth, in every engagement. The whole home can wait for chapter two.

Our beliefs

Four convictions we design by

Dusk is the truth

Rooms are judged at noon and loved at night. We design for the hour you actually live there.

Comfort is not a compromise

If it can't survive a Sunday afternoon nap, it isn't luxury — it's a showroom.

Provenance over price tags

A flea-market find with a story outranks an expensive object with a barcode.

Restraint is the flex

Anyone can add. Knowing what to leave out is where the years of craft go.
