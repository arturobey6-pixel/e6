Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

LuxuryRoomDesign ("we", "us", "our") operates https://www.luxuryroomdesign.com. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

  • Information you give us: name, email, phone number, and anything you include in consultation requests — including descriptions and photos of your home.
  • Project information: if you engage us, we additionally collect your address, floor plans, budgets, and preferences necessary to deliver design services.
  • Usage information: with your consent, analytics data such as pages visited, approximate location (city level), device type, and how you found us, collected via Google Analytics.

2. How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries and schedule consultations;
  • To design, manage, and deliver your project;
  • To send service messages (schedules, deliveries, appointments);
  • To send occasional journal updates only if you opt in — unsubscribe anytime;
  • To understand website usage (with consent) so we can improve it.

3. Photos of your home

Photos and plans you share are used solely for your project. We photograph finished rooms for our portfolio only with your written permission, and we never publish addresses or identifying details without separate consent.

4. Cookies and analytics

We use Google Analytics with Consent Mode v2. Analytics and advertising cookies are set only after you choose "Accept all" in our cookie banner; choosing "Necessary only" keeps them off. IP addresses are anonymized. See our Cookie Policy.

5. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We share it only with service providers who help us operate (scheduling, email, payments) and with trades and workshops engaged on your project (who receive only what the work requires), under confidentiality obligations, or when required by law.

6. Retention and security

We keep project records for the duration of our engagement plus the period required by law, then delete or anonymize them. Data is stored on access-controlled systems.

7. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing hello@luxuryroomdesign.com. California residents have additional rights under the CCPA; we honor verified requests regardless of state.
